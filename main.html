Be a part of this celestial spectacle Thursday June 13th at sundown around 6pm at Gold Medal Park, MPLS. Event is open to all ages, children 13 and younger must be accompanied by an adult.


Enjoy FREE POPCORN provided by the Minneapolis Parks and Recreation Board. Bring your own blankets to stretch out under the stars and witness the last visible meteor shower until 2034.


There will be a limited number of telescopes for a closer look courtesy of the Minnesota Science Center. For directions and maps click here.
